Aglio Olio

Description

Easy to make and delicious pasta

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Chilli Padi
  • Pasta
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Place Pasta into Boiling water
  2. Chop Garlic, Chilli Padi and Coriander
  3. Place Garlic and Chilli Padi into a pan with Olive Oil and heat
  4. Cook for 1-2minutes, drain pasta water
  5. Place al dente Pasta into pan
  6. Mix well and cook lightly
  7. Plate and serve with Coriander as garnish
