Luncheon Olio

Description

Easy to make and delicious pasta

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Chilli Padi
  • Pasta
  • Luncheon

Steps

  1. Place Pasta into Boiling water
  2. Chop Garlic, Chilli Padi and Coriander
  3. Cook Bacon till crisp, set aside to cool
  4. Place Garlic and Chilli Padi into a pan with Olive Oil and heat
  5. Cook for 1-2minutes, drain pasta water
  6. Place al dente Pasta into pan
  7. Mix well and cook lightly
  8. Chop up bacon and mix into pan
  9. Plate and serve with Coriander as garnish
